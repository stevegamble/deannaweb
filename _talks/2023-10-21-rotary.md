Objective:  Engage high school students by showcasing future career possibilities in analytics and how data and analytics skills can make a meaningful impact in healthcare. The presentation introduces how healthcare analytics supports better decisions in patient care, research, and operations, with a focus on self‑serve tools, real‑world use cases, and data democratization at The Ottawa Hospital.
