Objective: To demonstrate how synthetic data can accelerate research, quality improvement, and innovation in healthcare by enabling safe, privacy‑preserving, self‑service access to data, while outlining a practical risk and data‑sharing framework to support responsible data democratization across a health system, as presented to a national public health audience.

Referenced in an [article](https://www.oha.com/news/advancing-research-and-care-through-data-informed-insights) from Ontario Hospital Association.
